VuFind® is a discovery system designed and developed for libraries by libraries, and also suitable for building search interfaces to all kinds of content. The goal of VuFind® is to enable your users to search and browse through all of your resources in a single consistent and user-friendly interface. Content could include:

  • Catalog records
  • Institutional repository content
  • Locally hosted journal articles
  • Digitized library materials
  • Items available for interlibrary loan
  • Other collections and resources — just add metadata!

VuFind® is completely modular and includes tools to support a wide range of use cases, from a basic Library catalog search to a complex "Bento Box" incorporating data from many sources. It supports building a local index and/or integrating with a variety of existing third-party services. Since it's open source, you can modify or add modules to best fit your needs. A wide range of configurable options allow extensive customization without changing any code. An active and friendly community and a variety of commercial service providers mean that you can get support when you need it.
